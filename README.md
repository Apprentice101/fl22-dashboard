# FL-22 Voting Procedures Dashboard

**Florida's 22nd Congressional District** — open seat under HB 1-D map (2026 cycle).

## ⚠️ Redistricting nuance

Florida HB 1-D (signed May 4, 2026) renumbered several South Florida districts mid-decade:
- **NEW FL-22** = territory formerly known as **FL-25** (Wasserman Schultz's old seat), redrawn westward across the Everglades to include rural Hendry and Collier counties.
- **OLD FL-22** (Lois Frankel's Palm Beach seat) was renumbered **FL-23**.

The new FL-22 is an **open seat** with partisan lean ~R+9 to R+10. Wasserman Schultz resides in the new FL-22 (Weston) but announced May 22, 2026 that she is running in FL-20, not FL-22.

Equal Ground v. Byrd: FL Supreme Court denied emergency petition 6-1 on June 10, 2026; map locked in for 2026 midterms. First DCA appeal pending.
